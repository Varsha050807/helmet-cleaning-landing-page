The 3-Step Cleaning Process

Fully automated, deep sanitation that preserves the integrity of your helmet.

Step 1

Mist Spray Cleaning

High-pressure micro-mist removes dust, mud, and superficial particles from the helmet exterior and visor without causing scratches.

Step 2

Deep Sanitization

Advanced UV-C and ozone sanitization technology neutralizes 99.9% of bacteria, viruses, and odors inside the padding.

Step 3

Air Drying

High-speed, temperature-controlled airflow safely dries the helmet interior, making it immediately ready for your next ride.
